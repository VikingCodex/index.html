The Meadows

Welcome to Valheim. This is the safest biome.

  • Workbench: You need a Hammer (Wood) to build it. It must be covered by a roof to function.
  • Rested Buff: Never leave home without it. Sit by a fire inside your house to get it. It speeds up Stamina regen by 100%.
  • Tools: Prioritize a Flint Axe and a Bow.

You have 25 Health by default. You must eat to survive.

DANGEROUS

Black Forest

To progress, you need to make Bronze.

  1. Copper: Found in ground as massive mossy rocks.
  2. Tin: Found near water shores as small silver rocks.
  3. Smelting: You need Surtling Cores (found in Burial Chambers/Dungeons) to build a Smelter.
  4. Recipe: 2 Copper + 1 Tin = 1 Bronze.
  • Troll: Huge blue giant. Do not melee him yet. Use a Bow and kite him. His tree-club hits through walls.
  • Greydwarf Brute: Heals nearby enemies. Kill him first.
  • Skeletons: Guard dungeons. Weak to Blunt damage (Clubs/Maces).

Cultivator (Bronze): Allows you to plant Carrots. Farming is essential for food.

Bronze Axe: Allows you to chop Birch trees to get Fine Wood.

VERY HARD

The Swamp

⚠️ POISON WARNING

Before entering, you must brew Poison Resistance Mead in a Cauldron and Fermenter. Without it, Blobs and Leeches will kill you in seconds.

You are always "Wet" here, which reduces Stamina regen. Don't run too much.

Sunken Crypts: Use the Swamp Key (from The Elder) to open gates. Inside, mine "Muddy Scrap Piles" to get Iron Scraps.

Iron Gear: Provides a massive armor upgrade. Prioritize the Iron Pickaxe and Iron Mace.

  • Draugr: Undead vikings. Parrying them is key.
  • Abomination: Giant tree monster. Lure it over a Surtling Fire geyser to burn it to death quickly.
  • Wraith: Flying ghosts that appear at night.

Uncharted Lands Await

You have mastered the basics. Beyond the Swamps lie freezing mountains that require warm cloaks, and golden plains where death comes swift from the air.

Keep upgrading your gear, keep your food buffs active, and Odin will guide you.
